# Contributing to Clef

Welcome! Everyone is invited to contribute to Clef, regardless of experience, background, or location.  

Clef is an **entirely open-source ecosystem**. Everything is **contributable**, including:

- Code
- Architecture
- Specifications
- Design decisions
- Governance proposals

All contributions help shape the project and are treated with equal weight in discussions.

---

## Review Process

To maintain high quality and consistency:

- **All contributions require review** before merging.
- Reviews cover:
  - Correctness
  - Security
  - Architectural alignment
  - Code quality and readability
  - Compliance with project principles

> ⚠️ **Exception:** During early stages when a single contributor works alone, reviews are not required, but should be adopted as soon as more contributors participate.

---

## AI-Assisted Contributions

AI tools may be used to draft code, specifications, or documentation.  

**Rules for AI usage:**

- Any AI-generated output **must be thoroughly reviewed** by the contributor before submission.
- It must meet the same standards of quality, security, and readability as manual contributions.
- AI is a helper, not a substitute for understanding or correctness.

---

## Principles of Contribution

- **Transparency:** All changes and decisions are visible in the repository.
- **Equality:** Every contributor’s voice counts; all discussions are public.
- **Respect:** Critiques are constructive; disagreements are resolved via discussion.
- **Alignment:** Contributions must respect the architecture and privacy-first principles of Clef.

---

## Governance & Decisions

Decisions about:

- Architecture
- Protocol changes
- Design choices
- Policies

are **also open to contribution and review**.  
Every contributor may participate in these discussions on GitHub, in the same way as code contributions.

---

By contributing, you agree to uphold the quality, transparency, and privacy standards of Clef.  
Together, we ensure the project remains **open, secure, and sustainable**.
